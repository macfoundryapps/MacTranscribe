# MacTranscribe

**Type with your voice, 100% local & private.**

No servers, no signup, no data leaves your machine.

And did you know - Speaking is 5x quicker than typing.

MacTranscribe is your perfect partner for dictating and transcribing live speech to text.  It works with any App on your Mac.

Just speak, and we will paste what you said wherever your cursor is.  Simple.

There are 2 main ways to use MacTranscribe:

- Hold To Talk: Keep CMD+OPTION pushed down, and talk.  When you release the keys, your speech is processed and pasted into your current App
- Toggle Talk: Press CMD+OPTION+SPACE once, then talk, and press again to stop.

We find Hold To Talk is best for quick commands, and Toggle Talk is best for long dictation.

There are settings to choose your Microphone, quality versus speed, and even adjustments to help you clean up your speech to remove 'ummm, errr, hmmmm' and other bits and pieces.

**Key features**
- Native macOS application
- Dictation powered by Whisper or Apple
- Custom keyboard shortcut
- Works system-wide (wherever you can type)
- Excellent results in many languages
- No account required
- No subscription
